‚ú® Local Recommendations & Things to Do

Singapore is a vibrant city with endless activities! Here are a few recommendations for enjoying your time before or after the wedding.

Food & Nightlife

In Singapore, we are truly passionate about food! Make sure to dedicate time to trying our famous local cuisine.

  • Iconic Hawker Centres: Experience Singapore's food culture at **Newton Food Centre** and **Lau Pa Sat** for affordable, delicious local dishes.
  • Local Delights (Durian): For the adventurous, try the "King of Fruits"! Visit **Durian stalls** in Geylang or recommended spots for a true Singaporean experience.
  • Springleaf Prata: Visit a local chain like **Springleaf Prata** for delicious prata (Indian flatbread), a local favorite for breakfast or a late-night snack.
  • Clarke Quay: The main riverfront area known for its bustling bars, restaurants, and nightlife scene.

Iconic Attractions & Family Fun

  • Gardens by the Bay: Visit the Cloud Forest and Flower Dome, and catch the spectacular light show at the Supertree Grove.
  • Sentosa Island: Home to **Universal Studios Singapore**, S.E.A. Aquarium (Oceanarium), and beautiful beaches.
  • Wildlife Parks: Spend a day at the renowned **Singapore Zoo** and the unique **Night Safari** for an evening adventure.

Shopping & City Exploration

  • Orchard Road: Singapore's famous shopping belt for luxury and major brands.
  • Bugis Junction / Plaza Singapura: Great spots for accessible shopping and quick dining.

Nature & Outdoors

  • MacRitchie Reservoir: Enjoy scenic boardwalks and the famous TreeTop Walk (check for operating status).
  • Pulau Ubin: Take a ferry to this rustic island for a glimpse of Singapore's past and cycling trails.
